About Me

In business since 1998, I have more than 35 years of automotive skills that includes all aspects of automotive mechanical repairs; race car prep and team management; body, paint & frame repair; auto restorations; out-of-state Internet PrePurchase inspections; shop management; accident investigation; skiptracing and recovery. I cover the Central Texas area, generally in a 100-mile radius from Austin, but inspect vehicles in the San Antonio, Dallas and Houston area frequently. I am a former mechanic and body shop manager with a great amount of experience as a legal investigator with a technical advantage toward automotive losses. Accident investigations include detailed intersection light sequences and time-of-day variances, skid mark analysis and estimated speed calculations. I typically have a 48-hour turnaround time on auto losses, lease returns and warranty inspections. Heavy trucks, motorcycles, RV and boats may take a week. Turnaround time for other investigation varies on complexity and base info. Please email for additional information, complete menu and price list. JBHouse Business Services
